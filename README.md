### Hi there 👋

I'm a bioinformatician at **UCL**, working on omics pipeline development in **evolution and ecology**.
I build **reproducible, FAIR data pipelines** — mostly [Nextflow](https://www.nextflow.io/) / nf-core
workflows for sequencing and phenotypic data — and care a lot about making science others can re-run.

- 🧬 Background in phylogenetics, molecular evolution, and alternative splicing regulation
- 🔬 Hands-on with RNA-Seq, proteomics, genomic and phenotypic data across evo-devo & ecology
- 🛠️ Nextflow / nf-core pipelines · Unix · R · Perl · Python
- 🐝 PhD at the CRG (Barcelona); postdoc at UCL on wasp caste evolution; pipeline work at Seqera Labs

### 🌿 Eco-Flow

I lead **[Eco-Flow](https://eco-flow.github.io/)** — a
[BBSRC-funded](https://gtr.ukri.org/projects?ref=BB/X018768/1) community developing open-source
Nextflow pipelines for **agri-ecology**, working to close *"the omics gap in ecology"* with
reproducible, accessible and scalable workflows (built to nf-core standards, tested with nf-test).

**Pipelines** (a growing collection):

- [`genomeqc`](https://github.com/eco-flow/genomeqc) — assess & compare the quality of multiple genomes and their annotations
- [`synteny`](https://github.com/eco-flow/synteny) — synteny analysis across genomes
- [`excon`](https://github.com/eco-flow/excon) — quantify gene-family expansion & contraction
- DNA metabarcoding — [`dietary_amplicons`](https://github.com/eco-flow/dietary_amplicons), [`pollen-metabarcoding`](https://github.com/eco-flow/pollen-metabarcoding), [`nanoporemetabarcoding`](https://github.com/eco-flow/nanoporemetabarcoding)

**Training & community:** I organise [nf-core](https://nf-co.re/) hackathons, deliver multi-day
Nextflow and bioinformatics workshops, and am an active member of the nf-core community and UCL's
Advanced Research Computing (ARC) Centre.
